Delicious rib-eye steak with fries and peas.

Ingredients:
- Rib-eye steak 150-200g
- 1 Potato
- Frozen peas

Optional:
- Red peppers
- Salt
- Beef stock cubes

Preparation:
- Peal and slice the potato
- Fry for 5min. aprox.
- Fry rib-eye steak for 4min. aprox. each side
- Boil peas for aprox. 2-3min

Sauce:
- Chop red pepper
- Mix with 50ml of boiling water
- Add beef stock cube
